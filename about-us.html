About Us

Our purpose

The Pawsitive Society is a Chadwick International student-led club made to help abandoned dogs. Noticing that there are more and more dogs surrendered to shelters or abandoned every day, the club leaders, Claire Choi and Jiwoo Choi, decided to commence a club aiming to help the dogs in the dog shelters.

Our goal

Our club activities involve volunteering to take our club members to the Balradang Dogs Shelter for walking the abandoned dogs about 1-2 times a month, depending on the different conditions of the shelter. We also donate all of our profits made from fundraising at school to the Balradang Dogs Shelter, by purchasing necessities for the dogs. Additionally, in order to share the message, “Do not abandon dogs,” to our Chadwick community, we are leading campaigns twice a semester. Please join our club, and we are looking forward to meeting fellow dog lovers!
